Japanese Style Egg Sandwich

Needed Ingredients

  • Boiled Eggs
  • Kewpie Mayo
  • Black Pepper
  • Salt
  • Milk
  • Optional: little pinch of sugar

How To Make

  1. Put the boiled eggs in a bowl and smash evenly with a fork
  2. Pour in tbsp of milk in
  3. Pour in kewpie mayo, generous amount
  4. Add pinch of black pepper and salt and sugar if you want
  5. Mix together then place evenly on bread
